Three general sessions will powerfully address the issues we all face as parents, and a host of elective seminars will practically deal with your personal concerns. These fun, encouraging, and insightful sessions will provide helpful guidance for parenting.

Topics include:

  • Communication
  • Depression
  • Rebellion
  • Single Parenting
  • Eating Disorders
  • Peer Influence
  • Sexual Identity
  • Parenting Priorities
  • Influences of Pop Culture
  • Your Child’s Spiritual Life
  • Boundaries and Discpline
  • Other Stage Specific Issues

Along with our keynote speaker you will hear from kids themselves, professional counselors, and other parents as well. This conference will equip you to make the best use of your time as you parent in the present.

The conference will be held at Park Cities Presbyterian Church, March 23-25, 2006.
