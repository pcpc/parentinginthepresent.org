Parenting in the Present

Our Keynote Speaker

Len Teague

A dynamic communicator, Len Teague has been involved with youth and family ministry since 1973. After serving on the Young Life Staff for 12 years, he was Chaplain at The Baylor School in Chattanooga, TN, a private boarding and day middle-high school for 4 years. He has been on the staff at Lookout Mountain Presbyterian Church since 1990, and has taught at Covenant College since 1989. Len has been married to Shannon for 29 years and is the father of three and the grandfather of one.

You will find Len to be fun, authentic, and wise. His life matches what he teaches.

In 1978 he began the Fellowship of Christian Students Ministry in Macon. He served as director of Reach Out Ministries from 1985 – 86. After serving as student pastor at a couple of churches, in 1991 he and his family moved to North Atlanta to Perimeter Church (P.C.A.) where he served as the student pastor for eleven years. In 2002, Perimeter expanded his sphere of influence to oversee all ministries relating to all aspects of children, youth and family.

In addition to his ministry at Perimeter, in 1996, Matt began P.A.C.T. Ministry. This ministry exists to encourage youth pastors in their personal walk with God, family life, and strategic ministry.
He is a graduate of Covenant College and New Orleans Baptist Theological Seminary. Matt is a sought after speaker for student pastor conference, parenting conferences, and student conferences. He has written two books for student pastors, numerous student discipleship manuals and youth pastor training materials. He is currently working on a book to support parents in their investment into their teens. He exudes a great heart for students, parents, and youth pastors. He and his wife Vickie celebrated their 27th anniversary this year and have three children who are 24, 21 and 18.
